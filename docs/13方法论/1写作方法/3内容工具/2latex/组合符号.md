# 组合符号

组合符号(Combinatorics Symbols)
symbols|meaning|examples
--|--|--
n!|n factorial|$n!=n\times(n-1)\times ... \times 1$
$C_n^m$|Combination|$C_5^3 = \frac{5!}{3!(5-3)!}=10$
$P_n^m$|Permutation|$P_6^4 = 6 \times 5 \times ... \times 1 =360$
